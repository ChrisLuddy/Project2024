Welcome to the ACT Web Application

The Agentic Corporate Trader (ACT) Web Application is a dedicated platform for efficiently and accurately handling corporate clients, large brokerages, and investment firms. This platform gives you, as a fund manager, safe access to crucial client data, such as transaction histories, financial portfolios, and customized investment plans. Since every client represents a high-value business, the ACT Web App ensures that all of their data is current and easily accessible, giving you the ability to effectively manage their assets.

With the same features as ACT-Mobile, the ACT web application lets you easily monitor and manage client portfolios from any browser. You can actively track investments and make crucial decisions in real-time, ensuring that any portfolio you manage is optimized, whether using a desktop or mobile app.

This smartphone software has special features for fund administrators, including government organizations or big businesses. You can directly control high-growth assets by investing in up to 10 top technology equities and 3 cryptocurrency assets. The same powerful tools benefit fund managers and administrators alike, guaranteeing complete trading and portfolio management capabilities no matter where you are.

The ACT-AI Engine, available on the website and mobile app, is one of the notable features. You can make better, data-driven decisions with the aid of this AI-driven tool, which forecasts the future prices of 10 top technology stocks and 3 cryptocurrency assets. The same AI-powered forecasting capabilities are available to fund administrators as well, ensuring that managers and administrators have access to the latest data on investment strategies.

The platform's trade ratings system also assists in assessing the performance of your trades by providing you with AI-backed performance evaluations that take historical data, market patterns, and other factors into account. This provides the tools fund administrators and managers need to improve decision-making and optimize returns.

You can manage your clients’ investments with confidence and maximize their financial outcomes with ACT’s complete portfolio management, sophisticated AI forecasting, and trade review features. ACT provides all the tools you need to succeed, whether you're a fund administrator using the mobile app or a fund manager logging in online.
